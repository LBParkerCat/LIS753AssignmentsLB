We have 50,000 quality used (sometimes new) books, videos and more at our monthly (2nd weekend) sale. We offer items for adults and children. Prices start from $0.25 to $1, and there are Sunday specials. 100% of your purchase goes to the library. We are conveniently located at the Cubberley Community Center.

Hours vary by sale room, but all rooms are open 11AM-4PM SAT & 1PM-4PM SUN.

Our friendly sales people will help you find treasures in the stacks without sales tax. Need a present? Our gift certificates are available in $5.00 denominations. You can find more information about the Main sale, Children's sale, and Bargain sale by clicking the links.

Join us for fun and community...cats 🐱 will not be offered for sale or adoption.

Main sale

Our main sale offers adult books, CDs, DVDs, records, and magazines. Find the novel that transports you to another world, the travel book that inspires your next vacation, or the movie that moves you. Hardcover books start at $1.00 and paperback books start at $0.50. Main room sale hours are 11AM-4PM SAT & 1PM-4PM SUN. The Main Room faces East Charleston Rd: find it here. Check out our Bargain sale for even better deals!

If you want first crack at our awesome inventory on SAT, we use a ticketing system to ensure smooth traffic flow. We hand out up to 2 numbered tickets per person at the Main Room door, starting at 8AM. People come early to get in early, so don't be surprised if there's a ticket line well before 8AM. Your tickets reserve you places in line when the sale starts at 11AM. You are free to get coffee, run errands, or check out our Children's and Bargain rooms in the meantime. Be sure to get back to the Main room a few minutes before 11AM to join the line: something. No ticket, no problem! Just join the line and we'll let you in as soon as we can.

Why the numbers and line? We care about your health and safety, and the size of our main room limits occupancy to 160 people. Our ticketing system allows something. Our line system allows walk-up customers to enjoy something.

Children's sale

Our children's sale offers books, CDs, DVDs, and toys. We have picture books for youngsters, youth fiction & non-fiction for school-age kids, and many non-Enlish language books. Parents and teachers, we have special materials for you. Many prices are $0.50 or $1.00. Children's room sale hours are 10AM-4PM SAT & 1PM-4PM SUN. Note that the children's room opens 1 hour before the main sale on Saturday, so be early for the best selection.

The Children's Room faces the Greendell School and San Antonio Rd: find it here. While you're shopping early on SAT, you need to limit your selections to 12 items: just bring them to the cashiers to hold until you are ready to pay for all of your purchases. Not sure about what to buy for that grandchild or teacher? Our gift certificates (available in $5.00 denominations) make great gifts! Check out our Bargain sale for even better deals!

Bargain sale

Check out the bargain sale room for great deals, AND our Sunday specials! All adult hardback books are $1.00, paperbacks are $0.50, and children's books are only $0.25. Bargain room sale hours are 10AM-4PM SAT & 1PM-4PM SUN. Note that the bargain room opens 1 hour before the main sale on Saturday, so be early for the best selection. The Bargain Room is in Room H2, facing East Charleston Rd: find it here. Not sure about what to buy for that friend or relative? Donate

We accept donations from individuals, estates, and businesses. The Palo Alto Library generously donates items removed from their shelves, providing numerous newer materials. You may bring large donations to our Main Room or contact us to arrange a convenient drop-off time with assistance from our friendly volunteers

About us

Friends of the Palo Alto Library (FOPAL) is a member-supported 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the Palo Alto City Library and its users through volunteerism, book sales, and advocacy. Established in 1938, FOPAL has grown with the library community it serves. Our volunteers conduct monthly and online sales of books and other media donated by the community.
