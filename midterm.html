We have 50,000 quality used books, videos and more at our monthly sale. We have items for adults and children, including bargains. Prices range from $0.25 to $1, but there will be Sunday specials. 100% of your purchase will go to the library. Our friendly salespeople will help you find treasures in the stacks, and no sales tax.
