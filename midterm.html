Donate

We accept donations from individuals, estates, and businesses. The Palo Alto Library generously donates items removed from their shelves, providing numerous newer materials. You may bring large donations to our Main Room or contact us to arrange a convenient drop-off time with assistance from our volunteers

About us

Friends of the Palo Alto Library (FOPAL) is a member-supported non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the Palo Alto City Library and its users through volunteerism, book sales, and advocacy. Established in 1938, FOPAL has grown with the library community it serves. Our volunteers conduct monthly and online sales of books and other media donated by the community.
